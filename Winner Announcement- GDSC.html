Dear Insights & Data Colleagues,

 

Our latest addition to the Cool Place Program is the second global initiative, the Global Data Science Challenge: Your launch pad into the awesome world of AI and Data Science.

After several training programs covering the fundamentals of natural language processing and text classification, the participants had to build a context sensitive search engine for the sales team, i.e. a reference recommendation model which would take a natural language query and return a list of project references, sorted by predicted relevance. Over 430 colleagues from all over the world registered for the competition.

 

The challenge has now been concluded and we are very happy to announce the winners:
